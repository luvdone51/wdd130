Travel Rafting Fun

Travel Rafting Fun is a company that specializes in white water rafting adventures. We offer trips for all skill levels, from beginners to experts. Our experienced guides will take you on an unforgettable journey down some of the most beautiful rivers in the world. Whether you're looking for a relaxing float trip or an adrenaline-pumping whitewater adventure, we have the perfect trip for you. Contact us today to book your next rafting adventure!

History

Travel Rafting Fun was founded in 2025 by Kimberly Hendricks. Kim's love of the outdoors and passion for adventures led her to start the company with the goal of sharing her love for the thrill of rafting. Since then, Travel Rafting Fun has grown into one of the premier rafting companies in the world, with trips available on six continents. Our commitment to safety, customer service, and environmental stewardship has earned us a reputation as a leader in the industry.
