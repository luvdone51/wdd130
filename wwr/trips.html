History

WhiteWater Rafting Fun was founded in 2025 by Kimberly Hendricks. Kim's love of the outdoors and passion for adventures led her to start the company with the goal of sharing her love for the thrill of rafting. Since then, Travel Rafting Fun has grown into one of the premier rafting companies in the world, with trips available on six continents. Our commitment to safety, customer service, and environmental stewardship has earned us a reputation as a leader in the industry.
