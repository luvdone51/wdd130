Trips Taken

Family Fun

Perfect for all ages with gentle rapids and scenic views. We are at the Arkansas River's Bighorn Sheep Canyon. We chose a Class II route to give us a little taste of the rapids, but not enough to scare first time riders. It was such a fun experience

Beautiful Trails

Explore crystal-clear waters and lush rainforest surroundings. This trip we went down the Ruby Horsetheif Canyon, just off the Colorado River. It was such a beautiful and serene view, a musty see.

Fun for Everyone

From beginners to thrill-seekers, we have the trip for you. This trip is going down Gore Canyon in Colorado. It is a remote location, has rugged terrain, and exhilarating Class IV-V rapids. This was such a thrill seakingride, we faced intense rapids, drops and had such a great time.
